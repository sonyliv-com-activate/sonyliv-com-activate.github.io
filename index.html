Sonyliv.com/activate




sonyliv.com/active is a process that allows users to activate the SonyLIV streaming service on their devices. SonyLIV is an Indian streaming service that offers a wide range of TV shows, movies, live sports, and other content in various Indian languages, such as Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and others.


How to Register a Sony LIV Account?


To register a SonyLIV account, you can follow these simple steps:
  • Visit the Sony LIV website at sonyliv.com/active.
  • Click the "Sign Up" button in the upper-right corner of the homepage.
  • You can register using your email address or your mobile number. Enter your preferred option and click "Continue".
  • Fill out the registration form with your details, such as your name, email address, mobile number, and password.
  • Accept the terms and conditions and click on "Register" to complete the process.

    How to install SonyLIV on Smart TV?


    The installation process of SonyLIV on a smart TV is generally the same as the download process. Here are the steps for installing SonyLIV on your smart TV:
  • Turn on your smart TV and go to the App Store on your TV.
  • Search the SonyLIV app in the App Store.
  • Select the SonyLIV app from the search results and click the "Install" button.
  • Wait for the app to be installed on your smart TV.
  • Once the installation is complete, open the SonyLIV app and log in to your account or activate your device using the process at sonyliv.com/active.
